Tekken 8

What is this game?

Tekken 8 is a fighting game developed by Bandai Namco. Releasing on January 25, 2024, it features a 1v1 fighting space in a 3D environment. Tekken 8 introduces a new system called "Heat," which causes chip damage, new moves, and more. Tekken 8 is more focused on aggressive gameplay and pulling off long combos.

Thoughts so far

Tekken 8 is my multiplayer game (and only multiplayer game I've been playing) of 2024, and its been very fun and addicting. While I haven't played much recently, the graphics are very nice to look at, the story is fun, and the gameplay is hard but rewarding if you know what to do. I haven't played Tekken in a very long time, probably since Tag Tournament 2 in 2012, but Tekken 8 is my first Tekken where I took the time to actually learn the game. Very fun game.

Music Corner

The Tekken series is known for its loud and energetic music, taking inspiration from EDM, Rock, Synth, and many more. When fighting, there are two versions of background music (BGM), one version that plays during regular rounds (Normal), and the Final Round version (Climax).
